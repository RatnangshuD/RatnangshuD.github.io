Summary I am a Prime Minister’s Research Fellow at the Robert Bosch Centre for Cyber-Physical Systems (RBCCPS), Indian Institute of Science (IISc) Bangalore, working in the FOCAS Lab under Prof. Pushpak Jagtap. My research focuses on formal methods and scalable, disturbance-robust control for unknown nonlinear systems under complex temporal logic specifications.
